Calendar 2025
Kim Hoang Paintings are traditional Vietnamese folk prints originating from Kim Hoang Village, near Hanoi. Known for their bold red and yellow backgrounds, they depict everyday life, folklore, and nature with a rustic charm. “Xà Bông” Calendar was designed with a modern twist—featuring a snake intertwined with flowers that bloom each month, but still maintaining the main features of Kim Hoang paintings by its bold colors and illustration styles.

Heritage In A Bowl
For this project, I am transforming a Wikipedia page into a book design. I selected Vietnamese Cuisines as the topic and renamed it “Heritage in a Bowl”. To align with the theme, all food images in the book are presented in circular silhouettes. Additionally, I designed chopstick bookmarks to accompany the book.
